Frequently Asked Questions
Is my data private?
Yes. All data is stored on your device only. Nothing is ever uploaded to any server.
How do I import a bank statement?
Go to Transactions → tap the import icon → choose a PDF, CSV, OFX, or QFX file exported from your bank.
How do I scan a receipt?
Tap + to add a transaction, then select "Scan Receipt" and take a photo or choose from your library.
How do I export my data?
Go to Settings → Export as CSV to share your transactions with any spreadsheet app.
How do I delete all data?
Go to Settings → Delete All Data. This action is permanent and cannot be undone.
Privacy Policy
🔒 No data collected

Tally does not collect, store, or share any personal data. All transaction data remains on your device at all times. We do not use analytics, advertising, or third-party tracking of any kind.
